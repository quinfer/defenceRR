\begin{highlights}
\item Study extreme return and volatility spillovers among 21 global
A\&D companies.\item Spillovers intensified during the COVID-19 pandemic
and Russia-Ukraine war periods.\item Volatility spillover is high at the
upper quantile and exhibits low variability.\item Chinese A\&D stocks
offer diversification benefits under normal
conditions.\item Geopolitical risk drives both spillovers, especially
during crisis periods.
\end{highlights}
